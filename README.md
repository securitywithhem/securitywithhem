### 💼 Executive Summary

I am a Web Application Security Analyst, Penetration Tester, and B.Tech Computer Science student ('27) at Navrachana University. I approach cybersecurity not merely as a technical checklist, but as a critical driver of business continuity, startup survival, and user trust. 

My focus lies at the intersection of offensive security (**VAPT, API Security, OWASP Top 10**) and operational compliance (**GRC**). I bridge the gap between finding complex flaws (manual testing, IDOR, XSS) and defining robust, compliance-aligned mitigations that shield digital assets from real-world threat actors.
